All Features

Everything Your Family Needs

A complete care ecosystem — from daily medicine tracking to AI-powered health tools, all in one place

Medicine Reminders

Get timely Telegram alerts for every medicine. Mark doses as taken simply by replying YES. Track expiry dates and get interaction warnings for dangerous drug combinations.

Doctor Booking

Browse verified doctors filtered by speciality, experience, fee, and rating. Book home visits or online video consultations. View full profiles and patient reviews before booking.

Health Logging

Log BP, blood sugar, weight, pulse, and temperature daily. Get instant alerts when readings cross safe thresholds. View trend charts to track progress over time.

AI Lab Report Reader

Upload your lab reports (images or PDF) and get a plain-language summary — what was tested, what the values mean, and what to watch out for. No medical knowledge required.

Emergency SOS

One big red button sends instant Telegram alerts to all your emergency contacts. Request ambulance, track status from REQUESTED → ARRIVED → COMPLETED in real time.

Blood Donor Finder

Search for blood donors by blood group and location instantly. Register yourself as a donor with availability status. Connect families in need with willing donors nearby.

AI Food Recommendations

Get personalised diet tips based on your health conditions — foods to eat, foods to avoid, and general wellness tips. Helpful for diabetic, cardiac, and hypertensive patients.

Online Video Consultations

Doctors can toggle visits to "Online" and patients join a secure Jitsi Meet video room with one click. No app download required — works from any browser.

12+ Indian Languages

Switch between English, தமிழ், हिंदी, తెలుగు, ಕನ್ನಡ, and more with a single toggle. All UI labels and AI responses adapt to your chosen language automatically.

Step by Step

Get Started in Minutes

No complicated setup. No technical knowledge needed. Vaidyo is designed to be simple enough for elderly users and powerful enough for doctors.

Join Vaidyo
1
Register your account

Choose your role — Patient, Doctor, Nurse, or Caretaker. Fill in basic details. Doctors and nurses go through a quick council verification by the admin.

2
Link your Telegram

Connect your Telegram account using a simple command. This activates medicine reminders and emergency SOS alerts directly on your phone.

3
Add medicines & health data

Add your prescriptions, set reminder times, log daily vitals like BP and sugar. Upload a photo of the medicine or lab report if needed.

4
Book a doctor or use AI tools

Browse doctors by speciality, book home visits or video calls. Use the AI symptom checker, report reader, and food recommendation tools anytime.

5
Stay safe with Emergency SOS

In any emergency, press the SOS button. All your saved contacts get an instant Telegram alert. Book an ambulance and track its arrival status live.

AI Powered

Understand Your Health in Plain Language

Vaidyo's AI tools make complex medical information simple — no doctor degree required to understand your own health

AI Symptom Checker

Describe your symptoms in plain words. Get possible causes and general guidance — in English or your chosen Indian language. Always with a clear doctor-consult reminder.

AI Lab Report Reader

Upload a photo or PDF of your blood test, urine test, or scan report. The AI explains what each value means, what's normal vs abnormal, and what to follow up on.

AI Food Recommendations

Based on your health conditions logged in Vaidyo (like diabetes, hypertension, or heart disease), get personalised food advice — what to eat, what to avoid, and general diet tips.

⚠️ Important: All AI features provide general health information only. They are not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified doctor for medical decisions.
